At only 23, Becky Maslen has already carved out a hugely successful career in both modelling and television. 2011 promises to be an even bigger year for the 5'9" Yorkshire beauty, after this years success of her first ever E4 appearance. She looks set to continue a winning career over the next 12 months.

I was born in Bradford, England on the 27th June 1987 to David and Linda Maslen. I started out life dancing, running and getting into mischief whenever my parents turned their backs. We lived in Cullingworth, Bradford for the first 9 years of my life. Then we moved to Halifax, where I attended Ryburn Valley High school.

As I enjoyed my modelling work I decided to go to beauty school where I qualified as a beautician. I like to make people feel good about themselves!

I first started modelling at the age of 15 when I was scouted in my hometown of Halifax. I was quickly signed up and started modelling for various local companies. I then went on to working in television where I was a regular extra on ITV's Emmerdale Farm.

I settled down working for a company in Leeds - my first 9 till 5 job. Stuck at it for 2 years then decided it wasn't as fulfilling as modelling and television I was struck down by illness in late 2009 and after my operation decided I would try re-build my career as a model. 2011 looks to be a great year for me!
